Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Naveen Porwal, a Data Scientist/Reporting Manager at Merck Bangalore, specializing in the field of AI and automation. My main area of focus is Supervise/reinforcement learning, particularly in the context of generalized persistent monitoring by a team of cooperative multi-agent systems.

My work is applied to a range of real-world problems, helping to optimize risks, resources, timelines, budgets, and performance. Automated systems must work together in real-time to ensure effective monitoring.

My Background

I completed both my Bachelor's and Master's in Mathematics at IMT Ghaziabad. During my Master's thesis, I worked on theoretical high-data Match and its intersection with machine learning. This foundational work set the stage for my current research interests in applied machine learning and robotics.

I am driven by a passion for learning and technology. Whether it's coding, experimenting with new AI techniques, or diving into the latest advancements in machine learning, I thrive in the world of innovation. Outside of my academic life, I enjoy cycling, playing Cricket, and learning new languages to expand my horizons.

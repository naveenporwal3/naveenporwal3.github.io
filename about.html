Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Naveen Porwal, a Data Scientist and Reporting Manager at Merck Bangalore, with expertise in AI, automation, and end-to-end business intelligence (BI) solutions. My primary focus is on supervised and reinforcement learning, specifically for generalized persistent monitoring using cooperative multi-agent systems.

My work addresses real-world challenges by optimizing risks, resources, timelines, budgets, and performance. I design automated systems that collaborate seamlessly in real-time to ensure efficient and effective monitoring. I leverage a diverse tech stack, including Power BI and Tableau for advanced data visualization, Snowflake for cloud-based data warehousing, and Power Automate and Power Apps for streamlined workflows and custom applications. Additionally, I architect robust data architectures to support scalable and efficient business intelligence solutions, enabling data-driven decision-making for business analysts and stakeholders.

My Background

I hold a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Mathematics from IMT Ghaziabad. My Master's thesis explored the intersection of theoretical high-dimensional data analysis and machine learning, laying the groundwork for my current focus on applied machine learning, robotics, and data architecture.

I’m deeply passionate about technology and continuous learning. I work with a variety of tools, including Advanced Excel for complex data analysis, SQL for efficient database querying, and Python for building machine learning models. I also utilize Power BI and Tableau to create interactive dashboards for business analysts, and design data architectures using Snowflake for scalable data solutions. I actively contribute to automation projects using Power Automate and develop custom end-to-end BI solutions with Power Apps to empower organizations. Beyond work, I enjoy cycling, playing cricket, and learning new languages to broaden my perspective.

Personal Philosophy

I believe every day is a chance to grow. My goal is to be a better version of myself today than I was yesterday—whether through refining my research, embracing new technologies like cloud-based analytics, low-code platforms, or data architecture design, or personal development.

When I’m not working, you’ll likely find me cycling scenic routes, enjoying a game of cricket with friends, or honing new skills like building data-driven applications with Power Apps, optimizing BI solutions with Snowflake and Tableau, or exploring innovative data architectures to stay sharp and curious.

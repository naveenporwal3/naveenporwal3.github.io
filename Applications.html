Attendance System - FAQ

1. What is this system?

This is a biometric + QR-based attendance system to help record employee attendance easily and securely.

2. How does it work?

  • The kiosk displays a QR code.
  • Employees scan the QR code using their mobile page.
  • Attendance is verified and recorded in the system.

3. Is a selfie required?

No, attendance is verified only through employee QR scan.

4. What happens after scanning?

The system validates the session and confirms attendance with employee details and time slot.
